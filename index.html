About Us

At Fern Hill Animal Hospital, we believe that treating your pets with exceptional medical care shouldn't come at the cost of compassion. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced diagnostic tools, ensuring that we bring accurate and quick solutions to complex health problems.

Our Services

Cancer Care

Click to learn more

What it is: Comprehensive oncology services to diagnose and treat various cancers in pets.
Causes: Genetic predisposition, environmental factors, aging, and certain infections.
Symptoms: Abnormal lumps, non-healing sores, weight loss, lethargy, difficulty breathing or eating.
Our Approach: We offer chemotherapy, surgical removal, and palliative care tailored to improve your pet's quality of life.

Ophthalmology

Click to learn more

What it is: Diagnosis and treatment of eye diseases to prevent blindness and ensure comfort.
Causes: Infections, trauma, genetics, diabetes, or age-related cataracts.
Symptoms: Squinting, redness, excess tearing, cloudiness in the eye, pawing at the face.
Our Approach: We provide detailed eye exams, glaucoma screening, and cataract management.

Nutrition

Click to learn more

What it is: Diet planning and management to treat underlying diseases or maintain optimal health.
Causes: Poor diet choices, allergies, kidney/liver disease, or simple obesity.
Symptoms: Rapid weight gain/loss, dull coat, lethargy, digestive issues (vomiting/diarrhea).
Our Approach: Prescription diets, allergy trials, and personalized nutritional plans for every life stage.

Cardiology

Click to learn more

What it is: Specialized care for heart and cardiovascular conditions in animals.
Causes: Congenital defects, aging, breed predispositions, heartworm disease.
Symptoms: Coughing, fainting, exercise intolerance, rapid breathing, pale gums.
Our Approach: Echocardiograms, EKGs, blood pressure monitoring, and tailored medical management.

Neurology

Click to learn more

What it is: Diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.
Causes: Slipped discs (IVDD), tumors, infections, epilepsy, or trauma.
Symptoms: Seizures, paralysis, uncoordinated walking, head tilt, severe spinal pain.
Our Approach: Neurological exams, medical seizure management, and referrals for advanced imaging (MRI).
